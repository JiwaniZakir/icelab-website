The paper **Hidden Costs of Analog Deobfuscation Attacks** by V. V. Rao, K. Juretus, and Prof. Ioannis Savidis was published in the *IEEE Transactions on Very Large Scale Integration (VLSI) Systems*.

The work analyzes the security vulnerabilities and hidden costs associated with deobfuscation attacks on analog intellectual property protection techniques.
