Prof. Ioannis Savidis presented the tutorial **Machine Learning for Automated Physical Design** at the 2025 IEEE International Conference on Computer Design (ICCD) in Dallas, Texas.

The tutorial highlighted ML-driven methodologies for modern VLSI physical design flows developed in the ICE Lab.
