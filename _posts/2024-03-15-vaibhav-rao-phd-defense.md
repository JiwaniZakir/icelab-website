Vaibhav Venugopal Rao successfully defended his Ph.D. dissertation **Enhancing Analog Circuit Security Through Obfuscation** at Drexel University.

His thesis advances parameter obfuscation and security analysis techniques for analog integrated circuits developed in the ICE Lab.
